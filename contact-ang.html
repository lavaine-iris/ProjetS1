About us

My name is Iris Lavaine, I am currently in my first year of a BUT (Bachelor of Technology) Mmi (Multimedia and Internet professions).
As part of a pedagogical exercise, I had to create a website optimised for smartphones, specifically for the iPhone X. The subject was «from the Amazons to Balance your Pig» on the theme of feminism.
To answer this problem, I decided to deal with a topical subject which is the place of women in Afghanistan. The aim of my site is to raise awareness of the critical situation of women in Afghanistan since the return of the Taliban to power. Indeed, their rights are constantly regressing whereas they had managed to obtain them a few years ago.
